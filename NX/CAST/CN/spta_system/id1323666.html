You can reduce the number of DOFs (and consequently the number of equations) to a manageable number. System models support multiple superelement instances. The accuracy of your model, however, depends on your FE modeling and solver knowledge and experience.

At least three non-collinear boundary nodes are necessary in order to transform the superelement instance with respect to the assembly configuration.
