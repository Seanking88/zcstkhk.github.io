Supported system model solutions

The following solution types are supported for assembly FEMs containing external superelements:

  • SESTATIC 101 – Single Constraint, SESTATIC 101 – Multi Constraint (Linear Statics)

  • SEMODES 103 (Modal)

  • SEBUCKL 105 (Linear Buckling)

  • SEDCEIG 107 (Direct Complex Eigenvalues)

  • SEDFREQ 108 (Direct Frequency Response)

  • SEDTRAN 109 (Direct Transient Response)

  • SEMFREQ 111 (Modal Frequency Response)

  • SEMTRAN 112 (Modal Transient Response)
