Start the booklet wizard

Note

To access the Create Drawing Booklet wizard, you must not be in the Drafting application.

  1. Choose Preferences→Routing.

  2. On the Display tab, clear all of the check boxes.

  3. Click OK.

  4. Choose Tools→Drawing Automation→Create Drawing Booklet.

    The first step in the process is to provide a name for the booklet, along with a location where it is to be saved.

  5. First, make sure the Drawing Style is set to Assembly.

  6. In the Name box, type xxx Storage Facility, where xxx represents your initials.

  7. Click , then navigate to a writable location for your booklet, and click OK.

  8. Click Next to advance to the Introductory Template step.

    Introductory sheets typically contain general information about the drawing, such as assembly views and table of contents, revision blocks, construction and manufacturing notes, along with any tabular notes or parts lists.

  9. From the Introductory Template box, select:

    • A1 – Size (Millimeters)

  10. Click Next to advance to the Scope step.
