Enable the Large Assembly step

For the next series of views, you will utilize the Large Assembly step.

  1. Choose Preferences→Drafting.

  2. On the View tab, in the View Creation Wizard Large Assembly Option group, set the following value:

    Show when Component Count Exceeds

    =

    50

  3. Click OK.

    Now when you open the View Creation Wizard, the Large Assembly step appears in the Task Navigator below the Part step.
