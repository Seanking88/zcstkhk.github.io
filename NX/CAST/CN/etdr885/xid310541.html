Enhancements for view labels and section lines

What is it?

View labels and section lines are improved with the following enhancements.

  • Once all the available view letters have been used, you can add numbers instead of additional letters to a view label.

  • You can display secondary view label annotation as subscript or inline text.

  • You can display a rotation symbol and the rotation angle in the view label of a rotated section view or a rotated projected view.

    New angular precision options and angular format options can be applied to a view, and the values are also reflected in the view label.

  • You can now assign one of eight different font styles to section lines.

Why should I use it?

Use these enhancements to create standards–compliant section labels and section lines.
