Open the drawing with the assembly model unloaded

  1. On the Standard toolbar, click Open .

  2. In the Open dialog box, select the Load Structure Only check box.

  3. Double click drf7_85_eng_assm_dwg to open it.


    Sheet “Sheet 1” Work (Unloaded)

    The two views on Sheet 1 are smart lightweight views. The visible objects in them are extracted edges that were generated inside the views as they were being placed on the drawing sheet. Move your cursor over the border of each view to observe its view type:

    Smart Lightweight–Coarse Imported View : Top@1

    Smart Lightweight–Coarse Orthographic View : ORTHO@2

    The word ‘Coarse’ denotes the resolution quality for each view.
