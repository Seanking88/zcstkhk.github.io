Add a parts list to the drawing sheet

  1. On the Table toolbar, from the Table Drop-down list, select Parts List .

  2. Place the parts list at the upper left corner of the drawing sheet.

  3. Right-click the origin of the parts lists, and then choose Edit Levels.

  4. On the Edit Levels dialog bar, turn on , and then click .

  5. Right-click the origin of the parts list, and then choose Auto Balloon.
