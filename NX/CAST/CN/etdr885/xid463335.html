Select the view options

  1. In the Options group, from the View Boundary list, select Automatic.

  2. Clear the Show View Label check box.

  3. Click Next to advance to the Arrangement step.

  4. In the Arrangement list, make sure Arrangement 1 is selected.

  5. Click Next to advance to the Orientation step.

  6. In the Model Views list, make sure Front is still selected.

  7. Click Next to advance to the Layout step.
