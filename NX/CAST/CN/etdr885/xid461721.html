Create extracted edges in a pre-NX8.5 exact view

  1. Right-click the border of the upper pre-NX8.5 exact view, and then choose Style.

    EXACT (PRE-NX8.5)

  2. On the view General tab, in the View Configuration group, select the Extracted Edges check box.

  3. Click OK.

  4. In the Assembly Navigator, right-click the drf7_85_cyl_block node, and then choose Close→Part.

    EXACT (PRE-NX8.5)

    The extracted edges remain visible and selectable in the upper view. You can continue dimensioning the edges in this view.

  5. Close all parts.
