Create a sweep surface using the helix

  1. Zoom in on the square sketch with the rounded corners at the base of the vertical lamp rod.

  2. Choose Insert→Sweep→Sweep along Guide.

  3. Select the sketch.

  4. Click the middle mouse button to advance to the Guide group.

  5. Select the helix as the guide curve.

  6. Click OK.

  7. On the View toolbar, click See-Thru All .
