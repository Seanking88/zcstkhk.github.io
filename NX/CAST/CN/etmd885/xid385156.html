Locate the sketch at the lower isolated point

  1. Click Layer Settings and select the check box for Layer 21.

    You will now locate the arc center of the sketch at the lower isolated point.

  2. In the graphics window, right-click any sketch curve and choose Edit with Rollback.

  3. On the Sketch Tools toolbar, from the Dimension Drop-down list, click Parallel Dimension .

  4. Select the arc center of the circle.

  5. On the Selection bar, from the Selection Scope list, select Within Work Part Only.

  6. From the Selection Scope list, select Within Work Part Only.

  7. Select the Point of Isolate.

  8. Locate the dimension, type a value of 0, and then click the middle mouse button to complete the dimension.

  9. Click Finish Sketch .
