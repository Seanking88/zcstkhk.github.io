Specify Outputs

  1. In the Part Navigator, right-click Linked Part Module “Trigger Design” and choose Expand All.

    The Linked Part Module contains three collectors: Inputs, Work, and Outputs. Notice that it is the Inputs collector that has two WAVE linked features from the main part file.

  2. Right-click Linked Part Module “Trigger Design” and choose Activate Part Module.

    The third rib in the Work collector appears.

  3. Right-click Outputs and choose Specify Part Module Outputs.

  4. On the Selection bar, from the Face Rule list, select Body Faces.

  5. Select the face as shown.

    All the faces of the body are selected.

  6. In the Settings group, make sure the Deactivate after Specifying Outputs check box is selected.

    Click OK.

    In the Part Navigator, in the Outputs collector, notice that the third rib is added. The objects in the Inputs and Work collectors are hidden.
