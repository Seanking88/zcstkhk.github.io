Create the emboss body feature

  1. Open des18_85_emboss_body_5.

  2. Examine the underside of the part.

  3. In the Part Navigator, under Cameras, double-click “top-trimetric”.

  4. Choose Insert→Combine→Emboss Body.

  5. Select the large solid body.

  6. In the dialog box, in the Tool group, enter the following values:

    • Clearance = 4

    • Thickness = 5

  7. Select the smaller body.

  8. Click OK.
