Create draft on one side of the parting face

  1. Open des18_85_spanner_wrench.

  2. On the Feature toolbar, click Draft .

  3. At the top of the dialog box, click Reset .

  4. In the Type group, select From Plane or Surface.

  5. In the Draw Direction group, from the Inferred Vector list, select ZC.

  6. In the Draft References group, from the Draft Method list, select Parting Face.

  7. On the Selection toolbar, from the Face Rule list, select Feature Faces.

  8. Select the face of the extrude as shown.

  9. In the Faces to Draft group, click Select Face.

  10. On the Selection toolbar, from the Face Rule list, select Tangent Faces.

  11. Select the face as shown.

  12. In the Faces to Draft group, in the Angle 1 box, type 10.

  13. Click OK.

    Draft is applied above the parting faces.
