Draft both sides of a parting face

  1. Double-click the new draft feature.

  2. In the Draft References group, select the Draft Both Sides check box.

  3. In the Faces to Draft group, set the following:

    Symmetric Angle =

    Above Angle 1 = 7

    Below Angle 1 = 10

  4. Click OK.

  5. Hide the extruded parting feature so you can better observe the draft.

  6. Close the part.
