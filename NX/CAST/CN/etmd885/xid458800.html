Move the holes to the required locations

  1. In the Part Navigator, right-click Extrude (4), and choose Show.

  2. On the Standard toolbar, click Move Object .

  3. Select the point that was used to locate one of the positive holes.

  4. From the Motion list, select Point to Point.

  5. With Specify From Point active, select the arc center of the top of the threaded hole.

  6. With Specify to Point active, select the arc center of the top of the boss.

  7. Click Apply.

  8. Repeat steps 5 through 7 for the remaining 3 holes.

  9. Click Cancel.
