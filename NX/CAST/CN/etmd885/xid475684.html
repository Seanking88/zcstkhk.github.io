On your own

Swap the frame Linked Part Module for another design using what you just learned and the scenario in the following steps.

  1. Open des18_85_case_mount_2.

    Note

    You must open a Linked Part Module file before you can establish a relationship to it from the main design part file.

  2. In the Part Navigator, right-click Linked Part Module and choose Expand All.

  3. In the Inputs collector, double-click Linked Face (4).

  4. In the WAVE Geometry Linker dialog box, in the Parent Information group, notice that the Parent Part is des18_85_case_transmission.

    Even though the parent, case_transmission part file does not know its children, the children know their parents. This is because case_mount_2 was saved from the original case_mount_1.

  5. In the Part Navigator, right-click Linked Part Module and choose Make Main Work.

  6. Break the relationship to the frame mount shown.

  7. Establish the relationship to des18_85_case_mount_2.

  8. Be sure to delete the broken Linked Face.

  9. Unite the two Linked Part Modules to the solid of the main case.

  10. Merge the two Linked Part Modules into the main case_transmission part file.

    Note

    Linked Part Module files must be fully loaded to merge their features into the main design part file.

  11. Close the parts now or close all parts when you exit the session.
