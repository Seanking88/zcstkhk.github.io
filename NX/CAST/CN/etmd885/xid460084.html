Part Module

Estimated time to complete this lesson: 1 hour

This section covers how to design complex parts collaboratively using Part Modules in NX 8.5.

In this section, you will learn how to:

  • Prepare a single complex part for collaborative design by dividing the features into logical groups called Part Modules.

  • Create external Linked Part Modules to distribute different logical groups to different members of your team.

  • Keep the main part updated as work progresses in the different Part Modules.

  • Complete the design by merging the external Linked Part Module files into the main part file.
