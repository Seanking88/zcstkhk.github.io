Break a linked face relationship

  1. Open des18_85_case_transmission.

  2. In the Part Navigator, right-click Linked Part Module Frame Mount and choose Expand All.

  3. Examine the Part Navigator. Notice that the Outputs collector has one linked face which is WAVE linked from the original case_mount_1 Linked Part Module file.

  4. While the Linked Part Module Frame Mount is still selected, choose Format→Part Module→Break Relationship.

    The links are immediately broken and an Alerts message is displayed.

  5. Examine the Part Navigator. Notice that the Outputs collector has a broken linked face .
