Cap Face

  1. In the Part Navigator, right-click Datum Plane (1) and choose Show.

  2. On the Synchronous Modeling toolbar, click Delete Face .

  3. In the Settings group, make sure the Heal check box is selected.

  4. Select the six faces shown.

    The Alert messages provide clues to identify the error condition and offer potential solutions.

  5. In the Delete Face dialog box, in the Cap Face group, click Select Face .

  6. Select the datum plane as shown.

  7. Click OK.

    The six faces are deleted and the neighboring faces are trimmed to the datum plane.

  8. Close the part now or close all parts when you exit the session.
