Add features to a Part Module

A design change requires you to add blends to the current part. While you can add the blends after the Part Module is complete and united to the rest of the part, you might want to make your changes in the Part Module so that the features are contained within the Work collector.

  1. In the Part Navigator, right-click Part Module “Heat Sink” and choose Activate Part Module.

    The main body is hidden, and the Part Module is now the current feature. The objects in Inputs and Work collectors are displayed.

  2. Create a 2 mm blend on the two edges shown.

  3. In the Part Navigator, right-click Part Module “Heat Sink” and choose Deactivate Part Module.

    The Part Module remains the current feature.

  4. In the Part Navigator, right-click Unite (27) and choose Make Current Feature.

  5. Close the part now or close all parts when you exit the session.
