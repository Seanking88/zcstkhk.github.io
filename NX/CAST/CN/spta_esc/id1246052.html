NX Electronic Systems Cooling

The NX Electronic Systems Cooling is a comprehensive solver environment for the simulation of heat transfer and fluid flow in electronic systems and components. It includes all the capabilities that are available in the coupled thermal and flow solvers from the NX Thermal and Flow solver environment. Like NX Nastran, NX Electronic Systems Cooling is fully integrated into the native NX Advanced Simulation environment. Teamcenter tools are supported for controlling multiple design iterations and case studies.

NX Thermal and Flow DMP is an add-on product to all NX Electronic Systems Cooling products that removes any software limitations on the number of processes per run for parallel processing and enables parallel solutions over networks and clusters.

In addition to the NX Thermal and NX Flow features, NX Electronic Systems Cooling includes the following specialized tools:

  • Fan catalog

  • Thermostat and active heater controllers for heaters and fans

  • Peltier coolers, Joule heating, and electrical contact resistance

  • NX PCB Exchange — ECAD/NX associative data exchange

  • Parallel processing of the flow solver and thermal modules with access to up to 8 processes per run, on a single machine

The following additional tools are available with NX Electronic Systems Cooling if you have licenses to either or both Advanced Thermal and Advanced Flow:

  • Solar heating

  • Hydraulic duct networks

  • Humidity, condensation, and evaporation modeling
