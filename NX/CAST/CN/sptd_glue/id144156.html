Understanding the contact solution

For an NX Nastran solve, the augmented Lagrangian method is used. This method:

  • Includes a penalty stiffness to enforce contact constraints.

  • Enforces a zero penetration condition.

The solution is considered converged when there is zero penetration (the Contact Force Tolerance is satisfied) and the contact elements status change is below the specified criteria (the Percentage of Active Contact Elements is satisfied).
