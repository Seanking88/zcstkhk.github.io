Flange enhancement

What is it?

You can now create a flange on the edge of a deformed face formed by a Dimple feature. The edge of the deformed face must be linear, and the deformed face must be parallel to the original planar face on which the Dimple feature was created.

You can unbend or rebend the flange, or create a Flat Solid feature, or a Flat Pattern feature.

In the example a flange is created on the edge of a deformed face. The deformed face was created by an open section type of Dimple feature.

Where do I find it?

Application

NX Sheet Metal

Toolbar

NX Sheet Metal→Bend Drop-down list→Flange

Menu

Insert→Bend→Flange
