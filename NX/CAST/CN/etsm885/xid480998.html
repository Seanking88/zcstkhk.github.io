Add a dimple and create a bend through it

  1. On the NX Sheet Metal toolbar, from the Punch Drop-down list, click Dimple .

  2. In the Section group, click Sketch Section and create a rectangle similar to the one shown.

    The dimensions do not have to be exact.

  3. On the Sketch toolbar, click Finish Sketch .

  4. Click Reverse Direction so that the dimple feature is indented, not protruded.

  5. In the Dimple dialog box, set the following:

    • Depth = 4

    • Side Angle = 0

    • Depth Reference = Inside

    • Side Walls = Material Outside

  6. On the NX Sheet Metal toolbar, from the Bend Drop-down list, select Bend .

  7. In the Section group, click Sketch Section and sketch a curve across the dimple feature as shown.

  8. On the Sketch toolbar, click Finish Sketch .

    If you get the result shown, click Reverse Side to switch the side of the part that moves to create the bend.

  9. In the Bend Properties group, set the following:

    • Angle = 60

    • Inset = Outer Mold Line Profile

    • Extend Section =

  10. Click OK.

  11. Close the part now, or close all parts when you exit the session.
