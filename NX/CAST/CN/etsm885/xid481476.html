Create the louver

  1. Open nsm2_85_locker_front_door.

  2. On the NX Sheet Metal toolbar, from the Punch Drop-down list, select Louver .

  3. In the Louver dialog box, in the Cut Line group, click Sketch Section .

  4. On the outer non-flanged side of the part, sketch a curve and constrain it as shown.

  5. On the Sketch toolbar, click Finish Sketch .

  6. Set the following parameters for the louver feature.

    Louver Properties group:

    • Depth = 4

    • Width = 14

    • Louver Shape = Formed

    Rounding group:

    • Round Louver Edges =

    • Die Radius = 4

  7. If necessary, click Reverse Direction for Depth and Width to get the result shown.

  8. Click OK.
