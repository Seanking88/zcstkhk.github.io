The Orbit modeling object lets you define and visualize orbits. The Orbital Heating simulation object lets you define the faces that are being illuminated and the ray tracing method used to track this rays. You may use a Radiation simulation object to define internal enclosures.

By default radiation is calculated on the solar and infrared bands, however you can also define properties in other bands and calculate their thermal radiation exchange.
