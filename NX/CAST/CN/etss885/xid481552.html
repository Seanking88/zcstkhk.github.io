Microposition the poles

When you know a specific distance or value you want to move, rotate, or scale, you can use Microposition for finer control of the translation of poles.

  1. In the Curve or Surface group, in the Pole Selection subgroup, from the Manipulate list, select Poles.

  2. Click Select Object , then select all the displayed poles.

  3. In the Microposition group, in the Step Value box, type 10.

    This moves the selected poles 10 mm. in a normal direction from the faces.

  4. Click the negative button two or three times to create more convex surfaces.

  5. In the Settings group, click Restore Parent Face .
