Specify faces and choose an edit method

  1. Open des19_85_x-form_enhancements.

  2. Choose Edit→Surface→X-form.

    X-Form is also available on the Edit Surface toolbar.

  3. In the X-form dialog box, clear the Single Select check box.

  4. Select the two curved faces as shown.

  5. In the Method group, on the Move tab, click Normal.
