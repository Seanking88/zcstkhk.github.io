Specify boundary constraints

There are times when you will want to constrain the boundaries of an X-Form edit from moving, or constrain the boundaries to be tangent or curvature continuous.

  1. Expand the Boundary Constraints group.

    Notice that the U and V options are set to Free (No Constraint).

    This means that any pole movement is unconstrained in the U and V directions.

  2. In the Boundary Constraints group, set the following options:

    • Apply to All =

    • V-min = G0 (Position)

    • V-max = G0 (Position)

    Before (no constraints)

    After (V-min / V-max positionally constrained)

    Notice that only the poles available for edit are now displayed.
