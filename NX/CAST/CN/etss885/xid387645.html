Fit a spherical and a planar surface to facet bodies

  1. Rotate the part so you can see the underside.

  2. Choose Insert→Surface→Fit Surface.

  3. In the Fit Surface dialog box, from the Type list, select Fit Sphere.

  4. Select the interior spherical facet body.

  5. In the Fit Conditions group, clear the Closed check box.

  6. Click Apply.

  7. From the Type list, select Fit Plane.

  8. Select the planar facet body.

  9. Click OK.

    You now have all the fit surfaces necessary. The next step is to finish the blending of the fit surfaces.
