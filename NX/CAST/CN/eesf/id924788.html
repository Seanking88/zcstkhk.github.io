Replace the view and activate the two sections

  1. Choose View→Layout→Replace View.

    From the Replace View with... list select Isometric and click OK.

  2. In the Assembly Navigator, select the check boxes for both sections.

  3. In the Assembly Navigator, right-click Section 2 and choose Make Work Section.

  4. In the Assembly Navigator, clear the check boxes for both sections.

  5. On the View toolbar, click Clip Work Section to turn it off.

  6. Close the part without saving.
