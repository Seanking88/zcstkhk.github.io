Perspective-camera options

If you select Perspective from the Type list in the Camera dialog box, three extra groups are added to the dialog box:

  • Lens

  • Custom Zoom

  • Depth of Field

Lens

The Lens group defines preset zoom angle values for the given lens type.

The longer the lens, the narrower the field of view angle.

Zoom to = 135 mm

The shorter the lens, the wider the field of view angle.

Zoom to = 50 mm

Custom Zoom

The Custom Zoom group defines three parameters:

  • Magnification – sets the magnification without distortion.

  • Field of View Measured – lets you specify whether the field of view angle is measured horizontally or vertically.

  • Field of View Angle – defines the width of your view as an angle with its apex at your viewpoint and the ends at the sides of the view.

    When the Field of View Angle value is larger, you see more of your scene and the perspective becomes distorted; when the Field of View Angle value is smaller, you see less of your scene and the perspective flattens.

Depth of Field

When Depth of Field is enabled, you can set Aperture and Focal Distance values.

The results are only visible when you do a High Quality Image shading.
