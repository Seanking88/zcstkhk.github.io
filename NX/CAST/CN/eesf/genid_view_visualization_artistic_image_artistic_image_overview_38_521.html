Artistic Image overview


Artistic Image is used to apply non-photorealistic drawing effects to your model.

Each drawing effect can simplify or enhance the depiction of your model for illustrative purposes.

The purpose for non-photorealism is to filter out details that might distract a prospective customer from making a design decision.
