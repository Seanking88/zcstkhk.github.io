The Basic Lights dialog box

The eight lights are turned on and off by clicking on their respective buttons.

The intensity of each light is controlled by its slider or its input box.

Reset lights

Reset to Default Lights turns on the three default lights and resets their options to default values.

Reset to Scene Lights turns on all eight scene lights and resets their options to default values.

Scene dimmer

The Scene Dimmer slider increases or decreases the intensity of any lights that are turned on in the work view.

Note

The Scene Ambient light is not affected by the Scene Dimmer slider.
