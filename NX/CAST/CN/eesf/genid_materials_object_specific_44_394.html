Materials – Object specific

Clear Glass on front panel

Tan Texture on housing

Object specific materials can be applied on a per-solid or facet body basis.

They override any global material designation. Options include:

  • Glossy Plastic (various colors)

  • Metal (various)

  • Brushed Metal (various)

  • Metallic Paint (various colors)

  • Texture (various)

  • Glass (dark and clear), Rubber
