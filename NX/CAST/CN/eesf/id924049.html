Create a close-up camera based on an existing camera

  1. In the Part Navigator, double-click the front_upper_right camera to activate it.

  2. Right click front_upper_right and choose Copy.

  3. Right-click front_upper_right again and choose Paste.

    front_upper_right$1 is added to the list of cameras.

    The $1 suffix on the new camera indicates that it is a copy.

  4. Right-click front_upper_right$1 and choose Edit.

  5. In the Edit Camera dialog box, in the Name group, change the Camera Name to close_up_handle, then click OK.

  6. Rotate and zoom to view the handle.

  7. In the Part Navigator, right-click close_up_handle and choose Capture View.
