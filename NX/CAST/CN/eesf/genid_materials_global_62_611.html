Materials – Global

Low Sheen Plastic Wash depicted

Global materials apply to all objects in your displayed part.

They fall into two categories:

Washes

Normal object colors are used combined with reflection mapping. Options include:

  • Shiny Metal Wash (default)

  • Brushed Metal Wash

  • Glossy Plastic Wash

  • Surface Analysis Wash

  • Low Sheen Plastic Wash

Basic materials

Normal object color is ignored and a material color is used with reflection mapping. Options include:

  • Glossy Plastic (various colors)

  • Metal (various)

  • Brushed Metal (various)

  • Metallic Paint (various colors)

  • Glass (dark and clear), Rubber
