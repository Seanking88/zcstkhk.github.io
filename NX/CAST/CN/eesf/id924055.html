Use the camera staging window

  1. In the Part Navigator, double-click the Top camera to activate it.

  2. Right-click Top, and choose Edit.

    The staging window shows an orthographic scheme. This is because the camera was developed from an orthographic view.

  3. In the Edit Camera dialog box, set the Type to Perspective.

  4. In the graphics window, right-click in the background and choose Orient View→Trimetric to orient to a clearer view.

  5. Drag the ZC translation handle back and forth and watch the perspective change in the insert window.

  6. In the Custom Zoom group, in the Field of View Angle box, type 50 and press Enter.

  7. If your model starts to get clipped because of perspective settings, in the Clipping group, click Fit Planes to Extents to adjust the clipping planes.

  8. Click OK when you have a desirable top camera.

  9. Close the part when you finish.
