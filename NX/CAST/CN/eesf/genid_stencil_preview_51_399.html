Stencil Preview

Stencil Preview displays the resulting mask image that is used to produce a decal. All black pixels in the mask image result in a transparent surface, while all white pixels are visible.

You can work with the RGB Tolerance and the Stencil Preview to get just the right transparency for your stencil.
