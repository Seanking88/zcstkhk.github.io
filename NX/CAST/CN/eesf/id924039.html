Create a Stipple artistic image

  1. In the Artistic Image dialog box, click Stipple .

  2. Move the Dot Count slider to the maximum Dense setting.

  3. Click Start Shade .

  4. Click Erase Shade .

  5. Clear the Dot Color check box .

    When Dot Color is not applied, the dot color becomes the color of the individual components.

  6. Click Start Shade .

  7. Click Erase Shade .

  8. Click Reset to Default Options .

  9. Close the part.
