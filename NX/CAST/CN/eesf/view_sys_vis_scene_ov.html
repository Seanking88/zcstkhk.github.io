System Scenes

Use the System Scenes tab on the Resource bar to display a selection of visualization scenes that can be applied to change the background of your part.

Depending on the predefined scene you select, NX automates the effects that are specific to the scene.

After you drag and apply a scene into your view, use the Scene Editor dialog box to make further changes to your background, scenes, lighting, and reflection.

You can also save your own custom scenes in the System Scenes palette and apply it to your model.
