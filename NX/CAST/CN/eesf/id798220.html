Create a shaded background

  1. Open des07_true_shade_housing.

  2. Right-click in a blank area of a toolbar and select True Shading.

    The True Shading toolbar is displayed.

  3. On the True Shading toolbar, click True Shading .

  4. From the Global Materials Drop-down list, select Low Sheen Plastic Wash .

    Notice the default grey background of the True Shading environment.

  5. From the Background Drop-down list, select Inherit Shaded Background .


    Notice that the background is inherited from your session outside of True Shading.

  6. Click Show Shadows to disable shadows.

    This makes the floor reflection easier to see.
