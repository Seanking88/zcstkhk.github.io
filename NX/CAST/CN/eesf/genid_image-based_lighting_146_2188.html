Global Illumination – Image-Based Lighting

IBL works best with High Dynamic Range (.hdr) images but low dynamic imagery can also be used, but with loss of output quality.

High Dynamic Range images contain much more illumination information than conventional image formats; capturing all the real-world lighting information in a scene – from both natural and artificial sources.

Image-Based Lighting can use the following formats:

  • EXR and HDR (for high dynamic range images)

  • TIF, JPEG, LWI (for low dynamic images)

To edit advanced IBL parameters, such as IBL type, or color saturation, choose View→Visualization→Visual Effects→IBL.
