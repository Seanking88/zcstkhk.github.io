Rough Pencil


Rough Pencil makes your model look like it was drawn "freehand", with the lines sketched over and over for emphasis.

Using Rough Pencil, you can control the following parameters:

  • Outline Color — the color of the lines in the model

  • Outline Width — the thickness of the lines in the model; from Thin to Thick

  • Line Count — the number of lines in the model; from Few to Many

  • Line Deviation — the straightness of the lines; from True to Wobbly

  • Line Regularity — the smoothness of the lines; from Smooth to Jagged
