Illumination

Sometimes when decals and the base surface on which they are applied share the same material properties they can become hard to see if the base surface material is too reflective.

To solve the problem, you can set different illumination properties for your decals.

There are two types of decal illumination:

  • Inherit Material

    The decal inherits the material attributes of the surface on which it is being applied.

    • Base material = Gold

    • Decal material = Gold

  • Use Decal's Own Material

    You assign the decal a material type of its own.

    • Base material = Gold

    • Decal material = Plastic (nonreflective)

    When you select Use Decal's Own Material from the Illumination Type list, the Edit Illumination button becomes available. Press this button to access the Material Editor where different material types can be assigned.
