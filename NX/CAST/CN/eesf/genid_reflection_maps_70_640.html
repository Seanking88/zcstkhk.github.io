Reflection maps

Reflection mapping is a method of simulating a mirrored surface by means of a texture image.

The texture is used to store the image of the environment surrounding the object being rendered.

True Shading uses spherical environment reflection maps in which a single texture contains the image of the surrounding environment as reflected on a mirrored ball.
