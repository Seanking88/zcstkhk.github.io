Decal

Estimated time to complete this lesson: 19–32 minutes

In this part of the lesson, you will:

  • Identify images to be used as decals.

  • Place and rotate decals.

  • Use the different scaling methods.

  • Control the transparency of a decal for stenciling.

  • Edit the decal illumination properties.

  • Control decal displacement to create engravings.
