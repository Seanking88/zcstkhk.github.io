Apply a System Scene

  1. Open des07_teapot.

  2. Orient the view to Trimetric.

  3. On the Resource bar, click System Scenes .

  4. Drag the Granite Counter visualization scene into the graphics window.

  5. If necessary, in the Visualization Scene message box, click Yes.

  6. Fit the view.

    Depending on your current settings, your view may vary somewhat from the view shown here.
