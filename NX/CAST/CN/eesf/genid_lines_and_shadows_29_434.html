Lines and Shadows


Lines and Shadows lets you outline your model and color any shadows that may be applied to it.

Lines and Shadows has the following modifiable parameters:

  • Outline Color — the color of the lines in the model

  • Outline Width — the thickness of the outline; from Thin to Thick

  • Shadow Color — the color of the shadows

Note

Shadow settings are set up using High Quality Image and Advanced Lights.
