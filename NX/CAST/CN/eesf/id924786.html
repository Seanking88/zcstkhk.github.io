Activate each section

  1. In the Assembly Navigator, select the Section 2 check box.

  2. In the Assembly Navigator, right-click Section 1 and choose Make Work Section.

  3. In the Assembly Navigator, right-click Section 2 and choose Make Work Section.

  4. In the Assembly Navigator, right-click Section 2 and choose Unclip.

    Or, on the View toolbar, you can click Clip Work Section .

  5. In the Assembly Navigator, clear both section check boxes.
