Use Image Preferences to set up a partial shade area of your model

  1. In the High Quality Image dialog box, set the Method to Gouraud.

  2. Click Image Preferences .

  3. Select the Use Sub Region check box.

  4. Drag the smoothing slider to 1.50, then click Apply.

  5. Drag a rectangle around the sphere, then click OK.

  6. Click Start Shade .

    Notice that the shaded portion is cropped.

  7. On the Visualize Shape toolbar, click Erase Shade .
