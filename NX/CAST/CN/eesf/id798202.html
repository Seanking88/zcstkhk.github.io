Create a Flat image

  1. Open des07_method_test.

  2. Right-click in the graphics window and choose Rendering Style→Studio .

  3. On the Visualize Shape toolbar, click High Quality Image .

    Note

    High Quality Image is also available on the View toolbar.

  4. In the High Quality Image dialog box, from the Method list, select Flat.

  5. Click Start Shade.

  6. Aspects to note:

    • No texture mapping on flat surface.

    • Tessellation of the sphere feature.

    • Background color is truncated to one color.

  7. Click Erase Shade.
