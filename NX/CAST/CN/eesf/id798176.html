Create a Cartoon artistic image

  1. Open des07_doorlatch_persp_artistic.

  2. Right-click in a blank area in any toolbar and select Visualize Shape.

    A check mark appears next to the Visualize Shape entry in the list and the Visualize Shape toolbar is added and displayed.

  3. On the Visualize Shape toolbar, click Artistic Image .

    Notice that Current Style is Cartoon .

  4. In the Artistic Image dialog box, move the Outline Width slider to the thinnest setting.

  5. Click Start Shade .

  6. Click Erase Shade .

  7. Experiment with different Outline Width and Outline Color settings.

  8. Click Reset to Default Options .

    If you do not reset the defaults, any style settings will be remembered during your session.
