Section Plane

The Orientation of a 'base' section plane forms the basis from which other section planes can be referenced.

There are three Orientation plane designations that work in conjunction with the Set Plane options.

Absolute

Normal along the absolute x/y/z axis.

Located at the absolute axis origin.

WCS

Normal along the WCS x/y/z axis.

Located at the WCS axis origin.

Screen

Perpendicular to the plane of the screen and vertical/horizontal/normal.

Located in the middle of the bounding volume.
