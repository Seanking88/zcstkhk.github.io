Measure the distance between 2 bodies

  1. Choose Analysis→Measure Distance.

  2. From the Type list, choose Between Object Sets.

  3. From the Face Rule list, select Body Faces.

  4. Select any face of the tube.

  5. Click the middle mouse button to advance.

  6. From the Face Rule list, select Body Faces.

  7. Select any face of the panel.

  8. Zoom in and examine the value and location of the measurement.

  9. Click OK.

  10. Right click and choose Undo.
