Align Facets along Edges

What is it?

A new Align Facets along Edges check box is available. Select this check box to align the facets of bodies along a common edge by sharing the vertices of the facets.

Align Facets along Edges = off

Align Facets along Edges = on

When you translate NX files to JT, the aligned facets are also translated along with the material and texture applied to the facets.

Note

When the Align Facets along Edges check box is selected, the rendering time required to generate the facet edges may increase.

Why should I use it?

Use this option to get better quality rendering.
