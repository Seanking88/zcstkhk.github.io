Fit enhancements

What is it?

You can now set the following Visualization preferences for the Fit command:

Fit on Show or Hide

Fits your model to the view automatically after you use the Hide or Show command.

Note

  • This option applies to all the Edit→Show and Hide commands, except for the Immediate Hide command.

  • This option does not apply when the work view is a drawing.

Exclude Datums From Fit

Fits your parts to the view without taking datum objects into consideration.
