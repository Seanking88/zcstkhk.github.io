See-Thru Preview

What is it?

When you create or edit a feature, you can emphasize and better visualize the feature in the model preview by selecting the new See-Thru Preview Visualization preference. NX renders the important geometry, which is the geometry you are creating or editing, as a shaded display and de-emphasizes all other geometry.

See-Thru Preview turned off

See-Thru Preview turned on

You can customize the de-emphasis of less important geometry by changing one or more of the following Visualization preferences:

  • Translucency

  • Color

  • Edge effects
