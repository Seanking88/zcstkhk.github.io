Use the new Fit functions

  1. Open gwy1_85_view_fit_rotate.

  2. Choose Preferences→Visualization, and choose the View/Screen tab.

  3. In the Session Settings group, make sure the first six check boxes are cleared and then click OK.

  4. In the Part Navigator, right-click Datum Plane (40) and choose Show.

  5. In the View toolbar, click Fit .

  6. Choose Preferences→Visualization.

  7. On the View/Screen tab, in the Session Settings group, select the
    Exclude Datums from Fit check box, and then click OK.

  8. In the View toolbar, click Fit .

  9. Choose Preferences→Visualization.

  10. On the View/Screen tab, in the Session Settings group, clear the
    Exclude Datums from Fit check box, and then click OK.

  11. In the View toolbar, click Fit .

  12. In the Visualization Preferences dialog box, View/Screen tab, select the Fit on Show or Hide check box, and click OK.

  13. Hide the datum plane.
