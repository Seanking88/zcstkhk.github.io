Add two counterbore holes

  1. On the Feature toolbar, click Hole .

  2. In the Hole dialog box, from the Type list, select General Hole.

  3. In the Form and Dimensions group, from the Form list, select Counterbored.

  4. Enter the following values.

    • C-Bore Diameter = 5

    • C-Bore Depth = 0.5

    • Diameter = 2.5

    • Depth Limit = Until Next

    • Boolean = Subtract

  5. Select the centers of the two arcs.

    A preview of the counterbore holes is available.

  6. Click OK.

    The counterbore holes are created.
