Create the expressions

  1. Choose Insert→Sweep→Tube.

  2. In the Tube dialog box, click the Reset button.

  3. Select the center line.

  4. In the Cross Section group, set:

    • Outer Diameter = IPE_tube_od

    • Inner Diameter = IPE_tube_id

  5. In the Preview group, select Show Result .

    A preview of the tube is displayed.

  6. Click OK.

    The tube is created.

  7. Make the assembly file the Work Part.

  8. Close all parts without saving.
