Relocate the linked mirror part and then modify the source part

  1. On the Assemblies toolbar, click Move Component .

  2. In the Move Component dialog box, in the Transform group, ensure that Motion is set to Dynamic.

  3. Select asm2_85_housing_rt and click the middle mouse button.

  4. Drag the ZC translation handle approximately 40 mm in the negative direction to move the selected object as shown and click OK.

  5. Make asm2_85_housing_rt the work part.

  6. Right-click in the background of the graphics window and choose Orient View→Right.

  7. In the Part Navigator, select Sketch (1).

  8. In the Details panel of the Part Navigator, change the hole_xdist value to 9.

    The mounting stud in the source part and the corresponding counterbore hole in the linked mirror part move.

  9. Close all parts without saving.
