Custom visual reports

What is it?

A customization guide is now available to help you learn how to modify or create your own Visual Reporting report formats, bitmaps, and information view templates.

The guide is named guide.html.

Why should I use it?

Custom visual reports can help you quickly collect and display the particular information you want to review.
