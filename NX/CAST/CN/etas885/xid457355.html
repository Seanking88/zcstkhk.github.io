Remove the mounting studs

  1. On the Synchronous Modeling toolbar, click Delete Face .

  2. On the Selection bar, set Face Rule to Rib Faces.

  3. Select the two cylindrical faces of the mounting studs.

    The selected faces are removed.

  4. Select the two cylindrical faces of the general holes.

    The selected faces are removed.

  5. In the Delete Face dialog box, click OK.
