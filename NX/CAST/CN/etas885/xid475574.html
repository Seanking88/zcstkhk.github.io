Open the assembly structure

  1. On the Standard toolbar, click Open .

  2. In the Open dialog box, click Options.

  3. In the Assembly Load Options dialog box, in the Part Versions group, from the Load list, select From Folder.

  4. In the Scope group, from the Load list, select Structure Only.

  5. Click OK.

  6. Open asm2_85_demo_car_assy.
