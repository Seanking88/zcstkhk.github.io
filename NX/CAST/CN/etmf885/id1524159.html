Activity: Tilt the tool axis

Estimated time to complete: 8–12 minutes

When you machine against a wall or if you have clamps holding the workpiece, your tool and holder may collide with the wall or the clamp. To avoid this and still use the shortest possible tool, you can have NX automatically tilt the tool axis. NX replaces the colliding 3-axis motions with 5-axis motions to avoid the collision.

In this activity, you learn how to have NX tilt the tool axis to avoid tool holder collisions.
