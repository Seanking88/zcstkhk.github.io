Finish the floor and the walls more efficiently

  1. In the Path Settings group, click Cutting Parameters .

    The Cutting Parameters dialog box is displayed.

  2. Click the Multiple Passes tab.

  3. In the Output Passes group, make sure the Only Cut Along Wall and Floor check box is selected.

  4. Click OK to close the Cutting Parameters dialog box.

  5. Generate the operation.

  6. Verify the operation.

  7. Zoom in on the part.

  8. On the 2D Dynamic tab, move the Animation Speed slider to 5.

  9. Click Play .

    Notice that the operation finished the floor without wasted motion.

  10. Click OK to close the Tool Path Visualization dialog box.

  11. Click OK to close the Contour Profile dialog box.

  12. Close the part without saving it.
