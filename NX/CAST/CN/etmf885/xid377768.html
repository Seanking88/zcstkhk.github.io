Tool head management

What is it?

You can now mount tool heads to your machine tool model statically during a CAM session, or dynamically during CSE-based simulation.

You must create heads in NX and submit them to the device library. Simulation requires configuration of the post processor and CSE files.

A working example is provided in your ...\mach\samples folder.

Why should I use it?

In simulation, you can check if your tool path is correct and detect collisions between heads, other machine components, and the part.
