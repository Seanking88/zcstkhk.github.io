Increase the stepover feed rate

For this operation, you will use a rapid (G0) feed rate for transfer moves, and a stepover feed rate that is 50 percent faster than the cut feed rate.

  1. In the Path Settings group, click Feeds and Speeds .

    The Feeds and Speeds dialog box is displayed.

  2. In the Feed Rates group, review the following settings.

    Rapid

    • Output = G0 - Rapid Mode

    More

    • Step Over = 100 %Cut

    • Traversal = Rapid

  3. Change the Step Over feed rate.

    • Step Over = 150 %Cut

  4. Click OK.

    The Contour Profile dialog box is displayed.
