Activity: Compare IPW resolution settings

Estimated time to complete: 10–15 minutes

In this activity, you will do the following:

  • Review the IPW tolerance settings.

    You will not modify the tolerance settings because this requires re-starting NX.

  • Create a coarse resolution IPW facet body.

  • Create a fine resolution IPW facet body.

  • Observe the differences between the coarse and fine resolution facet bodies.
