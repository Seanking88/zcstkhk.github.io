Floor and Wall milling blank control

When you create Floor and Wall and Floor and Wall with IPW operations, you can use one of three options to define blank output.

  • The Blank Geometry option uses the blank defined in the Workpiece geometry group.

  • The 3D IPW option uses 3D IPW geometry from prior operations in the same geometry group.

  • The Thickness option lets you specify a blank thickness value to apply to the floors and walls, when the blank material is not defined in the part.
