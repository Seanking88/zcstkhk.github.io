Replay the existing operation

The FLAT_TOOL_NO_SHIFT operation profiles the part without shifting the tool axis.

  1. Open mnf16_85_ring_height_floor_passes.

  2. In the Operation Navigator, double-click the FLAT_TOOL_NO_SHIFT operation.

    The Contour Profile dialog box is displayed.

  3. In the Geometry group, in the Specify Floor row, click Display .

  4. In the Specify Walls row, click Display .

  5. Refresh the display.

  6. In the Actions group, click Verify .

  7. Press F8 to orient the view.

  8. Zoom in on the part.

  9. Select the tool path at the locations shown to see the tool placement.

    Notice where the tool contacts the wall of the part.

  10. Click OK.

    The Contour Profile dialog box is displayed.
