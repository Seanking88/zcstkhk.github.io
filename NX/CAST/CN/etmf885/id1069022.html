Use Preview and Display

  1. In the Preview group, clear the Preview check box.

    The tool path is displayed.

  2. Right click in the background of the graphics window and choose Refresh.

    The part is displayed.

  3. In the Preview group, click Display .

    The cut regions at each level and the IPW are displayed.

  4. Select the Preview check box.

    The cut region, cut levels, and the IPW are displayed.

  5. Click OK.

  6. Close the part without saving it.
