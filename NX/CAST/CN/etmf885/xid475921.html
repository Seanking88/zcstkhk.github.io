Review the current operation

  1. Open mnf16_85_ring_height_floor_passes.

  2. In the Operation Navigator, double-click the FLOOR_AND_WALL_FINISH operation.

    The Contour Profile dialog box is displayed.

  3. In the Geometry group, in the Specify Floor row, click Display .

  4. In the Specify Walls row, click Display .

  5. Refresh the display.

  6. In the Actions group, click Replay .

    The tool path has multiple depths.

  7. Click Verify .

    The Tool Path Visualization dialog box is displayed.

  8. Click the 3D Dynamic tab.

  9. Click Play .

    Notice that material remains on the floor.

  10. Click OK to close the Tool Path Visualization dialog box.

    The Contour Profile dialog box is displayed.
