Cross the wall gap with either a stepover or a transfer move

You can control when NX applies a transfer move by editing the Contour Profile drive method to change the Minimum Distance value.

  1. Open the Contour Profile Drive Method dialog box, and set the following:

    Across Wall Gaps group

    • Motion Type = Stepover

    Transfer within Region subgroup

    • Minimum Distance = 0.5 in

  2. Generate the tool path.

    Notice that the tool crosses the wall gap with different moves at the different locations.

    (1) When the gap is more than .5 inches, NX crosses it with a move to the common clearance plane.

    (2) When the gap is less than .5 inches, NX crosses it with a stepover motion.
