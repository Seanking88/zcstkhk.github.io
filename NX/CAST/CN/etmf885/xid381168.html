Preview a cut region and cut levels in Floor and Wall milling

When you create Floor and Wall and Floor and Wall with IPW operations, you can:

  • Display the Blank Geometry/3D IPW, cut levels and the cut regions continuously for critical levels by selecting the Preview check box.

  • Display the Blank Geometry/3D IPW and cut regions for all cut levels using the Display option.
