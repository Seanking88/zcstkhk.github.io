Floor and Wall milling using Extend Walls

When you create Floor and Wall and Floor and Wall with IPW operations, if the floor is not completely bounded by walls, use the Extend Walls option to control the cut region shape.

In the following examples, 1 = cut area floor, 2 = wall geometry, and 3 = cut region.
