Machine the top face of the part

  1. In the Geometry group, click Specify Cut Area Floor .

    The Cut Area dialog box is displayed.

  2. Select the top face of the part.

  3. Click OK.

    The Floor Wall dialog box is displayed.

  4. In the Path Settings group, from the Cut Pattern list, select Follow Part.

  5. Click Cutting Parameters .

    The Cutting Parameters dialog box is displayed.

  6. Click the Containment tab.

  7. In the Blank group, from the Blank list, select Blank Geometry.

    The Blank Geometry setting lets you preview the blank material.

  8. Click OK.

    The Floor Wall dialog box is displayed.

  9. In the Preview group, select the Preview check box to display the blank geometry, cut levels, and cut region for the operation.

  10. Clear the Preview check box.

  11. In the Actions group, click Generate .

  12. Click OK to save the operation.

  13. Close all parts without saving.
