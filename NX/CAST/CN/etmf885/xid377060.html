Volume based floor and wall milling

What is it?

Use the new Floor and Wall or Floor and Wall with IPW operation subtypes to efficiently machine prismatic parts and features. The Floor and Wall and Floor and Wall with IPW operations replace the Face Milling Area operation, and provide enhanced functionality.

When you use these new operations, you can:

  • Machine floors, walls, and floor and wall combinations simultaneously.

  • Machine walls and tapered walls that are not bounded by floor geometry.

  • Contain cut regions and control cut region shape.

  • Control the blank, and preview it and the cut levels.

Why should I use it?

Use the Floor and Wall operation for basic floor and wall milling on prismatic parts.

Use the Floor and Wall with IPW operation for floor and wall milling of prismatic parts when using an IPW to keep track of uncut material.
