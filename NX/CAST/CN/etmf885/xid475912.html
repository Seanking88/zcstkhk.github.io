Cross the wall gap with stepover moves

  1. In the Drive Method group, in the Method row, click Edit .

    The Contour Profile Drive Method dialog box is displayed.

  2. In the Across Wall Gaps group, set the following:

    • Motion Type = Stepover

  3. Click OK.

    The Contour Profile dialog box is displayed.

  4. In the Actions group, click Generate .

    Notice that the tool crosses the wall gap with a stepover motion at both locations.
