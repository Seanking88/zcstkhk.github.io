Check PMI associations to a hole

  1. In the Part Navigator, double-click the Front model view to make it the work view.

  2. On the Selection bar, from the Type Filter, select Edge.

  3. In the graphics window, select the larger diameter edge of the counterbore hole belonging to the existing PMI data.

  4. Right-click the selected edge and choose Find PMI Associated to Geometry.

  5. In the Find PMI Associated to Geometry dialog box, clear the Create Query Results View check box.

  6. Click OK.

    Unless the information feature has been turned off, NX displays an information window to inform you that a temporary display has been created.

  7. Click OK to close the information window.

    In the temporary display, only the PMI label directly associated with the outside edge of the hole is displayed.

    The two feature control frames and the note are associated only to the inside edge of the hole, and thus they are not shown in this temporary display.

  8. Press Ctrl+F to fit the display.

    NX discards the temporary display and restores the feature control frames and the note.

  9. Close all parts without saving.
