Translate the PCS to your teach point location

In this activity, you will show PMI notations and translate the PCS from its current MCS location to your teach point location.

  1. Show PMI data in the graphics window by doing the following.

    1. On the Resource bar, click Part Navigator .

    2. In the Part Navigator, expand the Model Views branch.

    3. Right-click PMI Assembly Filters and choose Add Filter→Display All.

    4. In the All Filter dialog box, from the Components to Use list, select All Components, and click OK.

    5. In the Part Navigator, expand the PMI Assembly Filters node.

    6. Right-click the filter you defined and choose Apply to Views.

    7. In the Apply to Views dialog box, in the Model Views list, select all the check boxes and click OK.

      PMI notations appear in the graphics window.

  2. On the Resource bar, click Inspection Navigator .

  3. On the Insert toolbar, click Alignment .

  4. In the Alignment dialog box, from the Type list, choose Translate PCS Origin.

  5. In the XP, YP, and ZP groups, from the Method lists, choose Measured Feature.

  6. In the XP, YP, and ZP groups, from the Select Feature lists, choose TEACH_POINT.

  7. In the PCS Name box, type PCS_TEACH_PT.

  8. In the Operation Name box, type TRANS_XYZ_TEACH_PT, and click OK.

  9. Save your inspection setup file.

The PCS is now translated to your teach point location.
