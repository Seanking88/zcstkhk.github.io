Complete your probe definition

  1. In the Probe dialog box, click OK.

    In Machine view of the Inspection Navigator, the new probe is now visible.

  2. (Optional) Choose File→Save to save your probe setup file.

  3. If you exported your probe to the default CMM Inspection Programming library, you can now test it in an inspection program by loading it as you would any other probe.

If your setup was correct, when inspecting an inner cylinder whose diameter exceeds that of your probe, the styli should measure the cylinder surface with the appropriate sensors.
