Create vertical constraints

  1. Open skt09_85_constraints_toolbar.

  2. Double-click the sketch as shown to open it for edit.

  3. Select the three lines as shown, and on the shortcut toolbar, click Vertical .

    To display the shortcut toolbar if you do not see it, right-click one of the selected objects.

  4. Select the three lines as shown, and on the shortcut toolbar, click Horizontal .

  5. Select the two lines as shown, and on the shortcut toolbar, click Convert to Reference .

    The shortcut toolbar is a convenient way to manipulate objects in the graphics window.

  6. Close the part now or close all parts when you exit the session.
