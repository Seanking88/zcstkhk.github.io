Sketch CSYS display enhancement

What is it?

In order to simplify the display while sketching, the internal datum CSYS no longer shows the sketch plane normal. The sketch internal datum CSYS is like any datum CSYS created, but when a sketch is active the sketch normal is no longer displayed.

NX8.0

NX8.5

Where do I find it?

Application

Modeling, Drafting, Shape Studio, Sheet Metal

Prerequisites

Sketch task environment, or while a sketch is active
