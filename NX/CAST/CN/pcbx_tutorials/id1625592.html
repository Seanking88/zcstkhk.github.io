Remove environment variables

For other activities or your daily work, you may not need the initialization files you defined. Remove the environmental variable that you set in a previous step from the system.

  1. Right-click on My Computer and select Properties.

  2. On the Advanced page, click on the Environment Variables button.

  3. Select MAYA_PCB_ENV_DIR.

  4. Click Delete.

  5. Click OK twice.

Restart NX for your next activity.

This step concludes the tutorial.
