Export your data from the navigator

You can save your connection data and import it into other parts.

  1. In the Electrical Connection Navigator, right-click and choose Export All.

  2. In Native NX, navigate to your student_home folder.

  3. For Teamcenter Integration, accept the default location.

  4. In the Files of type: box, make sure you still have Bookmark Files and PLM XML (*.plmxml) selected.

  5. In the File name: box, type ***_logical, where *** represents your initials.

  6. 点击确定。

  7. Close all parts without saving when you have finished.
