Add a column to the cut sheet

  1. Move your cursor over the right–most column so that it highlights.

    Tip

    If you experience any difficulty selecting the column, you can highlight just the column header cell, then right–click and choose Select→Columns.

  2. Right–click the highlighted column and choose Insert→Columns to the Right.

    A column is added to the cut sheet.

  3. Press Esc to deselect the highlighted column.
