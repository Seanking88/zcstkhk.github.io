Manually assign pin 33

  1. Right-click the MULTI_xxxxx node, and choose Assign Terminals.

  2. In the Terminal Name box, type 33 and then 回车。

    The number 33 appears at the bottom of the terminals list.

    It is still a virtual port at this time.

  3. Select 33 and then click Place Terminals.

  4. Make sure:

    Filter

    =

    Point

    Point Method

    =

  5. Select the inside edge of Counterbore Hole(45).

  6. Make sure the port originates at the base of the counterbored hole and points in the same direction as the other terminal ports.

    If it does not, click Cycle Direction.

  7. Click OK twice.

    Terminal 33 appears at the bottom of the expanded multi port node.

  8. 点击确定。

  9. Close the part.
