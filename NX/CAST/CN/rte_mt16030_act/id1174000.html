Generate a status report for the connections

  1. Open the Electrical Connection Navigator .

  2. Make sure the HARNESS node is expanded.

  3. To select all of the connections in a column, right-click any column heading and choose Select All.

  4. Under the HARNESS node, right-click any highlighted row and choose Status Report.

    Notice that there are a total of thirty connections and all were made automatically.

  5. Close the Information window.
