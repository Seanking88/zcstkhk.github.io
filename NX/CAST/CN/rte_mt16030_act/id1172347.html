Add a 33-pin connector to the back of the rotary switch

  1. Select Place Part .

  2. Click Open , and then double-click rte1_hl_33pin_conn.

  3. 点击确定。

  4. Select the port on the back of the rotary switch.

    Make sure both the Lock Engagement and Lock Rotation check boxes are selected.

  5. 点击确定。

    Every device in the assembly now has at least one connector attached to it.
