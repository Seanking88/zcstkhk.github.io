Add the same 4-pin connector to one connection in the controller

  1. Select the port on the second connector from the left on the bottom row.

  2. Make sure both the Lock Engagement and Lock Rotation check boxes are selected.

  3. 点击确定。

    Note

    Assembly Constraints are applied to the connectors as you place them in your assembly. If you wish to turn their symbols off in the view, open the Assembly Navigator , then right-click the Constraints node under the harness subassembly and clear the Display Constraints in Graphics Window check box.
