Interrogate the port problem

  1. In the Electrical Connection Navigator, select the W12 row.

  2. Open the Electrical Component Navigator.
    The two connectors used on wire W12 are highlighted, ANE and CBC.

  3. Review the Port Report for each of these two components.

    Observe that for the 16–pin connector (CBC), there are 16 ports, 2 unused ports, and 14 connections. That makes sense because there are only 14 wires going to the connector.

    However, look at the port and connection status for the eyelet (ANE). It has no ports defined. Therefore the problem is with the eyelet.

  4. Close the Information window.
