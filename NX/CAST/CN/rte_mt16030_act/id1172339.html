Attach a 24-pin connector to the processor receptacle

  1. On the Routing Electrical toolbar, from the Part Drop–down list, select Place Part .

  2. Collapse the Folder View box as you do not need it for this activity. This gives you more room to work.

  3. In the Select Existing Part group, click Open .

    All of the connectors you are going to select are the qualified ‘hl’ versions located in the parts directory.

  4. In the Part Name dialog box, double-click rte1_hl_24pin_conn.

  5. In the Specify Item dialog box, 点击确定。

  6. On the left of your Selection bar, from the Type Filter list, select Routing Objects.

  7. Select the port on the processor device.

  8. In the Part Options group, from the Reference Set list, select PORT.

    You also need to lock the connector’s engagement and orientation.

  9. In the Part Options group, under Settings, make sure both the Lock Engagement and Lock Rotation check boxes are selected.

  10. 点击确定。

    The connector is placed in your wiring harness subassembly.
