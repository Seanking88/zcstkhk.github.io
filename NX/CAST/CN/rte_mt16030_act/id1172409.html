Create a branch between the new RCP and the outermost meter

  1. Click Spline Path .

  2. On your Selection bar, turn off all Snap Point options except for .

  3. Select a point on the segment somewhere to the left of the clamp.

  4. On your Selection bar, turn off .

  5. Use to select the arc center points on both sides of the last hole in the bulkhead.

  6. Use to select the port on the small clamp.

    Tip

    The vector arrow always points to the direction that the segment will enter the clamp. If the arrow points in the wrong direction as shown above, right-click it and choose Flip Extension it to reverse its direction. If your segment ends up entering the wrong side of the clamp, go to the point list inside the Path Points dialog box, right-click the last point in the sequence (Point 4 in this case), and then choose Reverse Direction. To continue the path from that point, select New Point from the point list.
