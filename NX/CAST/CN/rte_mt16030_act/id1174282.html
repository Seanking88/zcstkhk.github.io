Edit the stock piece length

  1. In the Piece Length box, type 400 and 回车。

    The marker moves back a little from the end of the path.

  2. Click the middle mouse button.

    The Stock Browser dialog box lists the updated stock.

  3. From the Stock list, right-click Wrap_0152_mm and choose Info.

  4. Scroll a little ways down the Information window to see the stock’s characteristics: 

    ===============================
    Routing Characteristics
    THICKNESS          0.355600
    WIDTH              38.00000
    COLOR              Red
    MATERIAL           Vinyl
    OVERSTOCK TYPE     Wrapped
    PART_NUMBER        Wrap_0152_mm
    ===============================
    Object Characteristics
    LENGTH             400.00000

  5. Close the Information window.

  6. Click Close.

  7. Close all parts without saving.
