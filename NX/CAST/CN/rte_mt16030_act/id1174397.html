Activity: Create and orient a formboard

In this activity, you will:

  • Create a formboard using a wiring harness assembly similar to the one you worked on in previous activities.

  • Specify the layout for your formboard.

  • Orient and shape the harness branches.

  • Place the new formboard view on a drawing sheet.

Launch the Create and orient a formboard activity.
