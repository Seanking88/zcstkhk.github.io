Unassign the two meter connectors

  1. Under the HARNESS node, unassign the two meter connectors, MT1C and MT2C by selecting their green check marks to change them to symbols.

  2. Under the Work Part node, manually assign the two meter devices, MT1 and MT2. (MT1 is closest to the center of the front panel).

  3. Auto assign the two connectors, MT1C and MT2C by selecting their symbols. Notice how they change back to check marks.
