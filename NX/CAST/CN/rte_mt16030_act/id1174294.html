Open the drawer assembly

  1. Open rte1_drawer_assm_3.

  2. Right-click the bulkhead component and choose Hide.

  3. Make rte1_drawer_harness_3 the Work Part.

  4. Zoom in on the area of the 24–pin connector and the clamp on the base of the drawer.

    Notice that the clamps in this assembly display a fixture port at a stock offset point similar to the one you created in Lesson 2.
