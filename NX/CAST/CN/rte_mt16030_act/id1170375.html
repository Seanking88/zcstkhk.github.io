Review the connections in the Electrical Connection Navigator

  1. Open the Electrical Connection Navigator .

  2. Expand the HARNESS1 node.

    There are four wires in this connection list. The red not signs indicate that nothing has yet been routed.

  3. Click the Wire ID column header to sort the wires numerically.

    For this exercise, you used the Simple display instructions from the ugroute_elec.cfg file. The connection list display format for the Simple format includes these fields:
