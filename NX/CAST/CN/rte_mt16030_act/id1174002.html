Generate a status report for the components

  1. Open the Electrical Component Navigator .

  2. Make sure the HARNESS node is expanded.

  3. Right-click any column heading and choose Select All.

  4. Under the HARNESS node, right-click any highlighted row and choose Status Report.

    Notice that there are a total number of thirteen objects selected from the Component Navigator. Eight of them were manually assigned, the six 4–pin connectors and the two meter devices. The remaining five devices and connectors were auto assigned.

  5. Close the Information window.

  6. Close all parts without saving.
