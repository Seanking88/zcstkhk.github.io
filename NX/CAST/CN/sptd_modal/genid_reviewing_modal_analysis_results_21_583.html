Reviewing modal analysis results

Natural frequencies and mode shapes are the primary results for a modal solution.

  • The results are ordered by frequency, with the lowest natural frequency being the first mode, the next highest being the second mode, and so on.

  • The normal modes represent dynamic states in which the elastic and inertial forces are balanced when no external loads are applied.

  • The mode shapes represent relative displacement of the nodes.

  • The mode shapes help you determine what load locations and directions will excite the structure.
