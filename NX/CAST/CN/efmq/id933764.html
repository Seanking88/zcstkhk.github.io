Create the top surface

  1. Sketch the arc shown below on the Y-Z plane of the Datum CSYS.

    You can use Point on Curve and Tangent constraints to associate the sketch to the edges of the solid body.

  2. Click Extrude .

  3. Select the sketch.

  4. From the Start list, select Symmetric Value.

  5. In the Distance box, type 40 and click OK.
