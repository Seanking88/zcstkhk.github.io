Create a hole through the front left side of the plate

  1. Right-click the datum planes and choose Hide.

  2. On the Feature toolbar, click Hole .

  3. For the planar face to sketch, select the top face of the plate.

  4. In the Sketch Point dialog box, click Close.

  5. Constrain the point with dimensions at the location on the plate shown.

  6. Click Finish Sketch .

  7. In the Hole dialog box, in the Form and Dimensions group, from the Form list, select Simple and input the following parameters:

    • Diameter = 12.5

    • Depth Limit = Through Body

  8. Click OK.
