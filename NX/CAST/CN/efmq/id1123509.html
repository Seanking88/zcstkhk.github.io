Create sketch constraints

Create constraints to correct the thin section near the bottom of the part.

  1. On the Sketch Tools toolbar, click Geometric Constraints .

  2. In the Constraint group, click Fixed .

  3. Select the line shown to assign a Fixed constraint.

  4. In the Constraint group, click Parallel .

  5. Select the line shown.

  6. Click the middle mouse button to advance to the Select Object to Constrain to option.

  7. Select the line with the Fixed constraint.

The two lines are constrained together.
