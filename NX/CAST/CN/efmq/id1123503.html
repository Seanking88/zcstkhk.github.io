Review cross-section

You will review the cross section of the part parallel to the XC-ZC plane to view other changes that are needed.

  1. On the View toolbar, click Clip Work Section .

    Notice the problem areas.

    • 1. Thin section on the top and bottom of cavity

    • 2. Thin and non-parallel faces

    • 3. Deep notch

    • 4. Non-parallel faces

  2. Click Clip Work Section to turn off the section view.
