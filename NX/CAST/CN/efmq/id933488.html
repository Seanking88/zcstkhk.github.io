Create the first hole

  1. Click Hole .

  2. Click Reset at the top of the dialog box.

  3. From the Type Filter list on the Selection bar, select No Selection Filter.

  4. Disable all Snap Point options.

  5. Select the bottom face of the vessel for the sketch placement face in the approximate location shown below.

  6. Click Close in the Sketch Point dialog box.

  7. Constrain the point to be Point on Curve with the X-axis of the Datum CSYS and 230 mm from the Y-axis

  8. Click Finish Sketch .

  9. In the Hole dialog box, in the Form and Dimensions group, from the Form list, select Simple and input the following parameters:

    • Diameter = 50

    • Depth Limit = Through Body

  10. Click OK

    The hole is complete. It may appear on the opposite side of the Y-axis depending on where you initially selected the placement face. This is not important because you will be creating a circular layout of a pattern feature.
