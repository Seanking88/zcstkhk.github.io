Add chamfers

  1. On the Feature toolbar select Chamfer .

  2. From the Cross Section list, select Asymmetric.

  3. In the Distance 1 box, type 20.

  4. In the Distance 2 box, type 12 and press Enter.

  5. From the Curve Rule list on the Selection bar, select Single Curve.

  6. Select the upper circular edge of the base.

  7. Click Apply.

  8. If an alert appears stating that the chamfer cannot be created, click Reverse Direction and then click Apply again.

  9. In the Chamfer dialog box, from the Cross Section list, select Offset and Angle.

  10. In the Distance box, type 40.

  11. In the Angle box, type 20 and press Enter.

  12. Select the short edge on the top of the part.

    If the distances are measured in the wrong direction from the edge, click Reverse Direction to flip the chamfer.

  13. Click Apply.

  14. Using the same Cross Section and Distance, create another chamfer on the short edge on the other side of the part.
