Create a horizontal dimension

  1. On the Sketch Tools toolbar, from the Dimensions Drop-down list, select Horizontal Dimension .

  2. Select the two lines as shown.

  3. Locate the dimension and in the on-screen input box, type 240 and press Enter.

  4. On the Sketch Tools toolbar, click Finish Sketch .

  5. To turn off the display of the WCS, press W.
