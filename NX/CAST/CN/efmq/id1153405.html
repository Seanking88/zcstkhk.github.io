Add edge blends

  1. On the Feature toolbar, select Edge Blend .

  2. In the Shape list, make sure Conic is selected.

  3. From the Conic Method list, select Boundary and Rho.

  4. In the Boundary Radius 1 box, type 5 and press Enter.

  5. On the Selection bar, from the Curve Rule list, make sure Single Curve is selected.

  6. Select the upper circular edge of the chamfer on the base of the part.

  7. Click Apply.

  8. On the Selection bar, from the Curve Rule list, make sure Tangent Curves is selected.

  9. Select one of the edges at the intersection of the base and back extrusions.

  10. In the Boundary Radius 1 box, type 10 and press Enter.

  11. Click Apply.

  12. Select the two short, linear edges at the top of the part.

  13. Click Apply.

  14. In the Boundary Radius 1 box, type 1 and press Enter.

  15. Select one of the edges at the intersection of the pad and the base.

  16. Click OK.
