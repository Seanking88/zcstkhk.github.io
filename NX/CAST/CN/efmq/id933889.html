Add counterbored holes

  1. On the Feature toolbar, click Hole .

  2. Select the other side of the plate as the planar face and click OK.

  3. Create three additional points on the sketch plane.

  4. In the Sketch Point dialog box, Close.

  5. Constrain the three points as shown below:

    The lower point is centered along the length of the plate.

  6. Click Finish Sketch .

  7. In the Hole dialog box, in the Form and Dimensions group, from the Form list, select Counterbored and input the following parameters:

    • C-Bore Diameter = 35

    • C-Bore Depth = 12.5

    • Diameter = 18

    • Depth Limit = Through Body

  8. Click OK
