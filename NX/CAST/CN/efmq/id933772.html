Add the hole

  1. Click Hole .

  2. On the Selection bar, from the Type Filter list, make sure No Selection Filter is selected.

  3. On the Selection bar, make sure Arc Center is selected.

  4. Select the arc center of one of the 5 mm edge blends that you just created.

  5. In the Hole dialog box, in the Form and Dimensions group, from the Form list, select Simple and input the following parameters:

    • Diameter = 3

    • Depth Limit = Through Body

  6. Click OK.
