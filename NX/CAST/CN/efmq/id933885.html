Create a hole through the pivot boss

  1. On the Feature toolbar, click Hole .

  2. Select the right face of the pivot boss.

  3. In the Sketch Point dialog box, click Close.

  4. Constrain the point to be Point on Curve with both of the new datum planes.

  5. Click Finish Sketch .

  6. In the Hole dialog box, in the Form and Dimensions group, from the Form list, select Simple and use the following parameters:

    • Diameter = 60

    • Depth Limit = Through Body

  7. Click OK
