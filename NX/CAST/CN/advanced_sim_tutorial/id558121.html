Create the material record for the solar panels

The structure of the solar panels consists of a 0.5 in thick honeycomb core of quartz laminated with 0.005 in thick sheets of Quartz on the top and bottom. Create an orthotropic material record for the laminated material. Use mass density in the material record to account for the 0.5 in thick honeycomb core only. Use non-structural mass in the physical property record to account for the density of the 0.005 in thick sheets. Define the mechanical properties for the laminated structure on the material record.
