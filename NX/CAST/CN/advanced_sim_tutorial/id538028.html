Make the solid bodies coplanar

You will use a Synchronous Modeling command to modify the parts.

Synchronous Modeling makes it easy to make modifications regardless of the model's origin, associativity, or feature history. With Synchronous Modeling, you can use parametric features without the limitations of a feature history.

  Make Coplanar (Synchronous Modeling toolbar)

Note

You may need to turn on the display of the Synchronous Modeling toolbar. In the toolbar area, right-click and select Synchronous Modeling.
