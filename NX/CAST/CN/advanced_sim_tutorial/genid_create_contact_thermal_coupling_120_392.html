Create contact thermal coupling

  • Assume the chip is connected to the PCB using a Ball Grid Array (BGA) solder connection with a total area that is half of that of the chip.

    Assume as well a Pb-Sn weld of thermal conductivity 80 W/m-°C and a gap of 1 mm. You can now calculate a total conductance:
    Heat Transfer Coefficient =
    ksolder * Asolder / (lgap * Achip)
    Heat Transfer Coefficient =
    80·0.5/ 0.001·1 =40000 W/m2-°C
