Open the part

Before starting the tutorial, you must download the part.

  1. On your desktop or the appropriate network drive, create a folder named meshing.

  2. Click the Download link below:

    Download the part

  3. Extract the part file to your meshing folder.

  4. Start NX.

  Open

  • Navigate to the meshing folder you created.

  • File name

    Satellite.prt

  • OK

     

    The satellite is an assembly with two subassemblies: Satellite_Body and Equipment_Layout.

    The Satellite_Body consists of the outer body of the satellite and the four solar collector panels.

    The Equipment_Layout consists of the payload and the six boxes of equipment.
