Material Editor — Bumps tab

The options on this page let you control a material's bump mapping.

Bump mapping varies the surface normals to simulate bumps in the geometry.

Option

Description

Type

Specifies the type of bump mapping.

Cast (HQI only) type

Creates an irregular casting pattern using various-sized bumps and depressions.

You can modify the following parameters.

  • Scale: Controls the overall size of the bumps.

    You can enter a value in the Scale box, or use the up and down arrows. The up arrow doubles the scale, and the down arrow cuts it in half.

  • Casting Amplitude: Controls the basic amplitude or size of a bump in the cast surface.

  • Dented Amplitude: Controls the basic amplitude or size of a depression in the cast surface.

  • Dented Scale: Controls the overall size of the depressions in the cast surface.

  • Dented Threshold: Controls the relationship between the number of bumps and the number of dents.

  • Detail: Controls the amount of detail in the bumps.

Rough (HQI only) type

Creates a rough random texture on the surface.

In addition to the scale parameter, you can modify the following:

  • Rough Amplitude: Controls the overall amplitude, or size, of the roughness of bumps.

  • Detail: Controls the amount of detail in the bumps.

  • Sharpness: Controls the sharpness or roundedness of bumps.

Wrapped Dimple type

Creates a regular dimple pattern. Displays as a grid of spheres that protrude from the surface.

In addition to the scale parameter, you can modify the following:

  • Separation: Controls the overall roughness of the bumps on the surface. Controls the spacing between bumps as a factor of the radius.

  • Radius: Controls the radius of the bumps.

  • Center Depth: Controls the depth of the bumps as a percentage of the radius.

  • Blend: Specifies the look of the transition between bumps.

    For example, in the case of Wrapped Knurl, it changes the look from a pyramid to a cone.

Wrapped Rough

Creates a rough random texture on the surface that you can orient and stretch.

You can modify the scale, rough amplitude, detail and sharpness parameters.

Wrapped Image type

Creates a bump map with an image file.

You can modify the following parameters:

  • Softness: Controls the softness of the bumps.

  • Amplitude Controls the amplitude, or size, of the image file you choose for the bump map.

  • Image: Opens the Image File dialog box to let you to select a TIFF file for the bump map.

Wrapped Knurl type

Creates diamond shaped bumps.

In addition to scale and blend parameters, you can modify the following:

  • Amplitude: Controls the size of the diamonds in the bumps.

Wrapped Tread type

Creates a pattern of cylindrical bumps.

In addition to scale and blend parameters, you can modify the following:

  • Radius: Controls the radius of the cylinders in the bumps.

  • Amplitude: Controls the size of the cylinders in the bumps.

Leather (HQI Only) type

Creates the surface texture of leather using a solid displacement texture. The basic pattern is a series of cells of unit size which vary from square to irregular convex shape.

In addition to scale and rough amplitude parameters, you can modify the following:

  • Cell Amplitude: Controls the amplitude or size of each cell

  • Irregularity: Controls the irregularity of the cell size. A larger number creates more number of, and more irregular, or unequal-sized cells.

  • Smooth Min

  • Smooth Max

  • Rough Detail: Controls the roughness detail. A greater detail creates a more detailed bumped surface.

  • Rough Frequency: Controls the frequency, or occurrence of the roughness. A greater frequency creates a rougher surface.

  • Curve Amplitude: Controls the curvature of the bumps. A higher Curve Amplitude creates more curved, or wavy bumps.

  • Curve Detail: Controls the curve details. A higher Curve Detail creates more detailed curves.

  • Curve Frequency: Controls the frequency, or occurrence of curves. A greater frequency creates more curves.

  • Fold Amplitude: Controls the fold amplitude of the cells in the bumps. Higher the amplitude, more the number of folds.

  • Fold Detail: Controls the fold detail of the cells in the bumps. Higher the detail, more detailed the folds.

  • Fold Frequency: Controls the fold frequency of the cells. Higher the frequency, more the number of folds.

Wrapped Leather type

Creates the surface texture of leather. The basic pattern is a series of cells of unit size which vary from square to irregular convex shape.

The Wrapped Leather parameters are the same as Leather (HQI Only) parameters.
