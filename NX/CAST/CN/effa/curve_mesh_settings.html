体类型允许您指定片体或实体，对于实体，选定的曲线必须形成封闭环。

重新构建选项仅可用于法向构造选项。

Rebuild is available in the Section direction and the Lofting direction depending on the Output Surface Options choice, and whether or not the Preserve Shape check box is selected.

  • It is always available in the Lofting direction.

  • It is available in the Section direction for the Normal and Spline Points Output Surface Construction setting and if the Preserve Shape check box is cleared.

Rebuild constructs a high-quality surface by redefining the degree and/or segments of the sections. If the sections have different degree or segmentation, the output surface may be too complex or not smooth. This could prevent continuity between surfaces.

  • None – turns Rebuild off.

  • Degree and Tolerance — Rebuilds the surface using the specified degree. Higher degree generally reduces the chance of unwanted inflections and sharp changes in curvature. NX inserts knots as required to achieve the G0, G1 and G2 tolerance settings.

  • Auto Fit — Creates the smoothest possible surface within the specified Maximum Degree and Maximum Segments. NX tries to rebuild the surface without adding segments, up to the maximum degree.

    If the surface is out of the tolerance range, segments are added up to the maximum number you specify. If the surface is still out of the tolerance range, the surface is created and an error message displays.

There are four Tolerances. Not all are available at all times.

  • Intersection is the allowable distance between the Primary and Cross curves.

  • G0 (Position)

  • G1 (Tangent)

  • G2 (Curvature)

    The G1 and G2 continuity tolerances let you control the accuracy of the rebuilt surface in relation to the input curves. The rebuilt surface will not deviate from the input curves greater than the values specified for these tolerances.
