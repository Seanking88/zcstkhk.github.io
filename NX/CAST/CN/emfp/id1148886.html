Move the operation to a different location

You will move the ZLEVEL_PROFILE operation to the third position in the program.

  1. In the Operation Navigator, right-click ZLEVEL_PROFILE and choose Cut.

  2. Right-click CONTOUR_AREA_NON_STEEP and choose Paste.

  3. In the Operation Navigator, select PROGRAM.

    You will use dynamic material removal to visualize the tool path for the entire program.

  4. On the Operations toolbar, click Verify Tool Path .

    You could also right-click the operation in the Operation Navigator and choose Tool Path→Verify.

  5. In the Tool Path Visualization dialog box, click the 2D Dynamic tab.

  6. Click Play .

    Notice that the cut order now removes material more efficiently.
