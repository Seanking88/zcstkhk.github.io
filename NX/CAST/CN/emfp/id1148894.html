Compare the in-process workpiece to the part

Use dynamic material removal to visualize the tool path for the entire program, then compare the in-process workpiece to the part.

  1. In the Operation Navigator, select PROGRAM.

  2. On the Operations toolbar, click Verify Tool Path .

  3. In the Tool Path Visualization dialog box, click the 2D Dynamic tab.

  4. Click Play .

  5. Click Compare.

    Notice that the scallops have been removed from the top of the part.

  6. Click Cancel.

  7. Close the part without saving it.
