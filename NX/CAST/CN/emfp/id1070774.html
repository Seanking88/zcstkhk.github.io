Replay the program

You will animate the tool movement for the entire program, displaying material removal in 2D and showing the tool assembly.

  1. In the background of the Operation Navigator, right-click and choose Program Order View.

  2. Right-click again and choose Expand All.

  3. Click Program.

  4. On the Operations toolbar, click Verify Tool Path .

  5. In the Tool Path Visualization dialog box, from the Tool list, select Assembly.

  6. Select the 2D Material Removal check box.

  7. Move the Animation Speed slider to 1.

  8. Click Play .

  9. Click OK.

  10. Close the part.
