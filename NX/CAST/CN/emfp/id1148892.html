Specify a manual cut pattern to cut along the top of the walls

  1. Click Generate .

  2. In the Region Cut Patterns dialog box, double-click region_1_level_2.

    You will reposition the tool to a point in space just to the right of the corner of the part.

  3. At the top of the Create Manual Cut Pattern dialog box, be sure Reposition To Point is selected.

  4. Click to the right of the corner of the part.

  5. In the Create Manual Cut Pattern dialog box, from the list at the top, select Move To Point.

  6. On the Selection bar, make sure End Point is selected.

  7. From the Motion Type list, select Engage.

  8. Select the corner of the part as the engage point.

    You will create four linear cuts along the periphery of the top face.

  9. From the Motion Type list, select Cut.

  10. Select the four end points in the order shown below to define the sequence of linear cutting moves.

  11. From the list at the top, select Reposition To Point.

  12. Click near the center of the side of the part.

    You will engage the tool to the center of the side of the part.

  13. From the list at the top, select Move To Point.

  14. On the Selection bar, click Control Point.

  15. From the Motion Type list, select Engage.

  16. Select the center of the line as the engage point.

  17. From the Motion Type list, select Cut.

  18. Select the center of the line on the opposite side of the part.

    The path cuts across the center of the part to a point on the opposite side.

  19. From the list at the top, select Reposition To Point.

  20. Click near the center of the side of the part.

  21. From the list at the top, select Move To Point.

    You will engage the tool to the center of the side of the part.

  22. From the Motion Type list, select Engage.

  23. Select the center of the line as the engage point.

  24. From the Motion Type list, select Cut.

  25. Select the center of the line on the opposite side of the part.

    The tool cuts across the center of the part to a point on the opposite side.

  26. Click OK twice to complete the operation.

  27. Right-click, in the background of the graphics window, and choose Orient View→Isometric.

  28. On the View toolbar, click Shaded with Edges .

  29. In the Face Milling Manual dialog box, in the Actions group, click Replay .

  30. Click OK.
