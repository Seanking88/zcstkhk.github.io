Spot drill the 0.250 diameter holes

  1. On the Insert toolbar, click Create Operation .

  2. In the Create Operation dialog box, in the Operation Subtype group, click SPOT_DRILLING .

  3. In the Location group, set the options as shown.

    Program = PROGRAM

    Tool = SPOTDRILLING_TOOL

    Geometry = WORKPIECE

    Method = DRILL

  4. Click OK.

  5. In the Spot Drilling dialog box, in the Geometry group, click Specify Holes .

  6. In the Point dialog box, click Select.

  7. Click All Holes on Face.

  8. Click Maximum Diameter — None.

  9. In the Diameter box, type 0.600.

  10. Click OK.

  11. Select the two faces containing the holes.

  12. Click OK to complete the selection.

  13. Click End of Selection.
