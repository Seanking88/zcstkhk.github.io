Define the part geometry

  1. In the background of the Operation Navigator, right-click choose Geometry View.

  2. Right-click again and choose Expand All.

  3. Double-click the WORKPIECE node.

  4. In the Workpiece dialog box, click Specify Part .

  5. Select the solid body.

  6. In the Part Geometry dialog box, click OK.

  7. In the Workpiece dialog box, click OK.
