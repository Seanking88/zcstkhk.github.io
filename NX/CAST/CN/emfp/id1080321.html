Set the cut angle and stepover

  1. In the Contour Area dialog box, in the Drive Method group, click Edit .

  2. In the Area Milling Drive Method dialog box, in the Drive Settings group, in the Percent of Flat Diameter box, type 10.0.

  3. Click OK.

  4. In the Contour Area dialog box, in the Actions group, click Generate .

  5. Click OK to complete the operation.
