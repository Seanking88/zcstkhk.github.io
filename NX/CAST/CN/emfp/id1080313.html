Apply the trim boundary to remove the edge tracing

  1. In the Operation Navigator, double-click CONTOUR_AREA.

  2. In the Contour Area dialog box, in the Geometry group, from the Geometry list, select MILL_AREA.

  3. Click OK.

    In the Geometry view of the Operation Navigator, the CONTOUR_AREA operation should now appear under the MILL_AREA node.

  4. In the Operation Navigator, right-click MILL_AREA and choose Generate.

    Notice that the tool does not roll over the edge.
