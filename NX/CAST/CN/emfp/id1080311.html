Define a trim boundary within a geometry group

To remove the edge tracing, you will create a MILL_AREA geometry group that contains a trim boundary. The boundary is defined on the bottom face of the part.

  1. On the Insert toolbar, click Create Geometry .

  2. In the Create Geometry dialog box, in the Type group, from the list, select mill_contour.

  3. In the Geometry Subtype group, click MILL_AREA .

  4. In the Location group, from the Geometry list, select WORKPIECE.

  5. Click OK.

  6. In the Mill Area dialog box, in the Geometry group, click Specify Trim Boundaries .

  7. In the Trim Boundaries dialog box, under Trim Side, select Outside.

  8. In the graphics window, select the bottom face of the part.

  9. Click OK twice to complete the MILL_AREA geometry group.

    The MILL_AREA geometry group should now appear inside the WORKPIECE group in the Geometry view of the Operation Navigator.
