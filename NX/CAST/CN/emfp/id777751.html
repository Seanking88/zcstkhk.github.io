The Planar and Cavity Milling project

Estimated time to complete this lesson: 21–31 minutes

In these projects, you will create programs consisting of Planar and Cavity milling operations that machine the parts using standard machining techniques. You will then check each program by dynamically displaying material removal and comparing the finished machined part to the original model.

You will use the Verify command to graphically simulate material removal and compare the in-process workpiece to the part.
