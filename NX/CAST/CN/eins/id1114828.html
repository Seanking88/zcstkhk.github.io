Uninstalling CAST

To remove the CAST Online Library:

  1. Log onto your workstation with an Administrator account.

  2. Open a Terminal window, then use a standard rm command to remove the CAST and .app directories. For example:

    • rm –r /Applications/Siemens/NX8.5/ugdoc

    • rm –r /Applications/Siemens/NX8.5/NX8.5_Apps/ugdoc.apps

You can also simply use Finder to move these folders to the Trash.
