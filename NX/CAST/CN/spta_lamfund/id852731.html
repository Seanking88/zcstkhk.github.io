Note

In the Laminate properties worksheet, you have solver and laminate specific properties listed.

In the Material worksheet, you have the material information listed.

In the Ply Material worksheet, you would have the ply material information listed if it is used.

In the Laminate Definition worksheet, you have the ply number, material, material type, thickness, and angle listed.

In the Laminate Equivalent Properties worksheet, you have the [A], [B], [D], [S] stiffness matrices and the laminate equivalent properties.
