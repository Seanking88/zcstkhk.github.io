Align the top of the headset

You can use the Move Face command to move a face in the direction normal to that face. In this step, you will move faces to align the top face of the headset part with the bearing cup near the top of the headset.

  Move Face (Synchronous Modeling group)

  •  

  •     drag the Distance arrowhead up 7mm

  • Apply

Leave the Move Face dialog box open for the next step.
