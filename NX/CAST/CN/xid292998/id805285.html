Activity: Model bolted connections

You will learn how to:

  • Use a CBEAM element to represent a bolt and use RBE3 spider elements to connect the ends of the bolt to the surrounding mesh.

  • Apply a pre-load force to the bolt.

  • Create a second subcase so that the combined effect of bolt pre-load and service loads can be compared to bolt pre-load alone.

  • Examine the stresses acting on the cross section of the bolt.
