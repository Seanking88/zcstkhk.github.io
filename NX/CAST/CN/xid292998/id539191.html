Apply constraints to the sides of the model

Individual integrally bladed rotors are joined by welding the outer rims together. This construction suggests that the rims of individual rotors should be modeled as plane strain. To do so, constrain the axial displacement of the outer rim.

Because of the nodal coupling in this model, you need to exclude the edges of the faces that also serve as edges of faces containing the dependent nodes.
