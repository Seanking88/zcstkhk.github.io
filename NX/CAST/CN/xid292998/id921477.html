Examine the material view

In previous steps, you used the Simulation Navigator to determine how the meshes are assigned to the collectors. By switching the Simulation Navigator to Material View, you can identify the material assigned to each collector, mesh, and polygon body.

In this activity, materials are assigned directly to the collectors. By expanding the No Material node, you see that materials are not assigned to the polygon bodies. Because each collector has an assigned material and each mesh is assigned to a collector, it is not necessary to have materials assigned to the polygon bodies.
