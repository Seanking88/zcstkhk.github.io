Activity: Flexible body analysis -- Advanced Simulation tasks

You will learn how to:

  • Create a SOL 103 - Flexible Body solution in Advanced Simulation and specify a body as flexible.

  • Connect the flexible body finite element model to the degrees of freedom in the motion mechanism.

  • Solve the finite element model and generate the RecurDyn Rflex input file.

Note

To complete this activity, you must have the nx_motion_rflex license, in addition to Motion Simulation, Advanced Simulation, and NX Nastran.
