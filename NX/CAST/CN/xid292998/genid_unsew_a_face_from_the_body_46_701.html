Unsew a face from the body

  Rotate the part to view the underside.

In the following graphic, the rectangular face   is not sewn at the outer edges because it has no edges in common with the other faces. The inside circular edge   is sewn; you can verify this by repeating the Sheet Boundary check in the Examine Geometry command. Because the inside circular edge is shared with another face, this rectangular face is part of the larger sheet body.

To remove this face from the body, unsew it and then suppress it in the Part Navigator.
