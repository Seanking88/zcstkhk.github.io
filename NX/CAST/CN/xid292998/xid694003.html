Techniques for applying the bolt pre-load

This activity shows three different techniques for applying a bolt pre-load to a bolt modeled as a mesh of solid elements. The bolt pre-load is applied to a cross section through the body of the bolt.

You will define the bolt pre-load:

  • On a set of polygon faces resulting from a split body operation.

  • On the nodes related to split body faces.

  • On nodes related to elements that define a plane on the bolt shank.

You should avoid defining the bolt pre-load on locations where the bolt geometry changes significantly, such as where the body of the bolt meets the head of the bolt.
