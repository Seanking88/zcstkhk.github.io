Display new surfaces from mesh mating

In a previous step, you displayed the gray surface of the middle body to show that it was one continuous surface. The mesh mating step has divided that surface into three surfaces.

  Simulation Navigator

  •     Polygon Geometry (expand)

  • Turn off the display of the inner bodies to see the surfaces created by the mesh mating step.

  •     Polygon Body(3) (deselect)

  •     Polygon Body(4) (deselect)

  • Notice that there are now three surfaces on the middle layer, as shown by the highlighted middle surface.

  • Turn on the display of the inner bodies.

  •     Polygon Body(3) (select)

  •     Polygon Body(4) (select)
