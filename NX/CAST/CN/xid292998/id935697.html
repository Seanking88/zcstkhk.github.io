Stitch the FEM file

In preparation for meshing, use Stitch Edge to eliminate any gaps between surfaces and overlapping internal free edges.

  Stitch Edge (Polygon Geometry group)

  • Automatic

  • The Automatic method can stitch both free edges to other free edges, as well as free edges to free faces, based on the Geometry To Stitch setting.

  • (Select Body)

  •   Drag a rectangle around all midsurfaces.

  • OK

     

  • The model is now ready for meshing.
