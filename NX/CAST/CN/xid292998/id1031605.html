    Note that the objective of the analysis was to minimize the weight, but it appears that the weight has increased. The optimization algorithm works in the overall scope of the objective, yet it must satisfy the constraints. The algorithm increased the shell element thicknesses to keep the displacement and stress constraints in range, but did so as minimally as possible.
