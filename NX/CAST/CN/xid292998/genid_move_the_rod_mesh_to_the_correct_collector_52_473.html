Move the rod mesh to the correct collector

In a previous step, you were instructed to create the solid mesh for the rod in the wrong mesh collector. Now move it to the correct collector. Prior to moving the rod to the proper collector, you will change the display color of the Steel collector so that the change of collectors is more evident.
